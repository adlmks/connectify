FAQs

What insights can I get from the Analytics page?

The Analytics page provides data on your profile's engagement, including the number of profile views, likes, comments, and shares. You'll also see detailed reports on the performance of your posts over time.

How often is the data updated on the Analytics page?

Data on the Analytics page is updated in real-time. You can track your social activity and interactions with other users as soon as they happen.

Can I export my Analytics data?

Yes, Connectify allows you to export your Analytics data as a CSV file, so you can analyze your performance further using external tools or keep records for your reference.
